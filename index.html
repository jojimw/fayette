About



  • 270,000+ maternal deaths occur worldwide annually, 99% of which occurred in low and middle-income countries and 14% occurred due to hypertensive disorders.
  • Nearly 10% of all pregnancies are complicated by hypertension.
  • Hypertension contributes to 1/5th of pre-term births and 1/4th of still-births globally.
  • Complications from high BP in pregnancy have become more common; increasing from 5.3% of delivery hospitalizations in 1993 to 9.1% at present.

Fayette Innovations designs and develops cutting edge technology for pregnant women to monitor their BP and other vital parameters continuously and non-invasively, regardless of their socio-economic status.
